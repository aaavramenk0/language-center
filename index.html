Course basic

English for Young Learners courses are designed to teach English to children and teenagers in an age-appropriate and engaging way. These courses aim to make learning fun and interesting for young learners and to foster a love of language and learning.

Some common topics covered in English for Young Learners courses include:

  • Basic vocabulary and grammar, such as colors, numbers, and basic sentence structure
  • Pronunciation and intonation
  • Listening and speaking skills, such as following directions and participating in class discussions
  • Reading and writing skills, such as writing simple sentences and reading short stories
  • Vocabulary and language related to familiar topics such as family, school, and daily routines
  • Games and activities to make learning fun and interactive
  • Songs and rhymes to help with memorization and pronunciation

Business English

Business English courses are designed to help students improve their language skills specifically for use in a business setting. The main focus of these courses is to equip students with the vocabulary, grammar, and communication skills they need to succeed in the global business environment.

Some common topics covered in Business English courses include:

  • Business vocabulary and terminology
  • Correspondence and document writing
  • Business communication skills, including meetings, presentations, and negotiations
  • Business culture and etiquette
  • Reading and analyzing business texts, such as financial reports, business plans, and articles
  • Business-related grammar and sentence structure

Course PRO

English for Academic Purposes (EAP) courses are designed to help non-native English speakers improve their language skills specifically for use in an academic setting. These courses aim to provide students with the language, study skills, and cultural knowledge they need to succeed in an English-speaking university or college.

Some common topics covered in EAP courses include:

  • Vocabulary and grammar specific to academic texts and discussions
  • Reading comprehension and critical reading strategies
  • Research and report writing skills, including citation and referencing
  • Presentation skills and giving academic presentations
  • Note-taking and summarizing skills
  • Listening comprehension and note-taking strategies
  • Discourse and interaction in academic settings
  • Cultural knowledge and expectations in academic settings

English for TOEFL or IELTS

Test preparation courses for exams like TOEFL or IELTS are designed to help students improve their English language skills specifically for these standardized tests. These courses aim to familiarize students with the format, content, and requirements of the test and provide them with strategies and practice opportunities to perform their best on the test day.

Some common topics covered in test preparation courses for TOEFL and IELTS include:

  • Review of grammar, vocabulary, and idiomatic expressions;
  • Familiarization with the test format and question types;
  • Strategies for answering different types of questions;
  • Practice tests and simulations to give students an idea of what to expect on the test day;
  • Tips and tricks for time management and test-taking strategies;
  • Evaluation and feedback to help students identify and improve their weak areas.
